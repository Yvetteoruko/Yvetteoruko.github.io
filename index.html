About Yvette Oruko

Design

Based in Nairobi, Yvette Oruko is a designer and illustrator with a particular interest in branding and editorial design. Passionate about building cohesive and strong brands as well as typography and packaging, she’s applied a touch of new-fashioned elegance to her works. Her major skills lies on the following softwares: Adobe Illustrator, Adobe Photoshop, Adobe XD and Corel Draw.

User Experience Design

Yvette also has skills in prototyping/wireframing mostly using Desktop prototyping tools such as UXpin and Mockflow which offer UI flow mode and they help in making work easier and faster.
